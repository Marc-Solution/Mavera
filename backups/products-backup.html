Lavender

Not only will this Lavender deodorant keep you smelling fresh all day. It can also make you feel better. Research suggests that lavender oil, which is in this deodorant, may be useful for treating anxiety, insomnia, depression and restlessness.

Ingredients: Organic lavender oil, spring water, bicarbonate

Eucalyptus

Not only will this Eucalyptus deodorant keep you smelling fresh all day, it can be good for your health. Eucalyptus oil, which is in this deodorant, can enhance your immune system.

Ingredients: Organic eucalyptus oil, spring water, bicarbonate

Citronella

Not only will this Citronella deodorant keep you smelling fresh all day, it can be good for your blood pressure. A 2012 study, published in the Journal of Health Research, reports that inhaling citronella vapors may significantly reduce a person’s blood pressure, heart rate, and respiratory rate.

Ingredients: Organic citronella oil, spring water, bicarbonate
