About

After hearing about the harmful effects of using aluminium in deodorants, we started looking for natural deodorants. We discovered that it was not so easy to find in Stockholm, Sweden where we live. So we decided to make our own natural organic deodorant. This is how this journey started. MaVera Natural Organic Deodorants was created by Carolina Vera and Marco Deb in the summer of 2020.

Mission & vision

We care about the planet earth and all its habitants. So it felt natural for us to make it our mission to help people live healthier lives, and also help the planet to be healthy. We will always use 100% organic and natural ingredients in our products.

Our Vision is to take care of this planet and its inhabitants, the best way we can, so that we create a better world for us and future generations to come. Our intention and goal is to always give good service to people and to the planet. We want to assist all of humanity in our collective desire to switch over to living nature-friendly healthier lives. We believe that together we are strong. Together we CAN create a better world for all us.
